What Tech Solutions and Setup Covers.

Real estate businesses in Pakistan operate with significant manual overhead including inquiries managed in WhatsApp, properties tracked in spreadsheets, follow-up done from memory, and no visibility into what is working and what is not.

Bitoreal Tech Solutions and Setup replaces this manual overhead with simple, functional technology including a website that generates leads, a CRM that tracks every inquiry, and systems that give you visibility into your business without requiring a full-time IT team.

We select, configure, and document everything so your existing team can operate it independently from day one.

What’s Included
  • Website development for lead-generating sites for real estate agencies and developers
  • CRM system selection, setup, and configuration
  • Property inventory system setup and configuration
  • Lead management workflow design and implementation
  • Digital process documentation for operational staff
  • Training for your team on all implemented systems
What’s Not Included
